Your Mobile Assessment App

At Industrial Training Services, we believe in creating streamlined, easy-to-use tools to meet your training and compliance needs and help instill confidence that company and regulatory standards are being met. That’s why we’ve built OnBoard® Assure, a mobile app to help you better determine the strengths and weaknesses of your team out in the field, in the office—wherever assessments are needed!

Build

Build digital, interactive documents with OnBoard® Form Builder and tie these forms and checklists to OQs as well as workflows, internal training, inventory audits, annual reviews, and more! By using custom, company-specific forms, you can ensure your team is following your company’s policies, safe practices, and hold the correct qualifications for the work being performed.

Assess

Auditors can use these custom forms right away inside the Assure app. As they proceed through an assessment from the convenience of a phone or tablet, they can flag exceptions and document resolutions in real-time, helping you meet federal regulation and company policies.

Report

All assessment data is available in our industry-leading OnBoard® LMS. Create and share Assure reports with the click of a button. These reports can be accessible on the job site or in the office, whether you’re walking around with an auditor or meeting with your team. Assure keeps an accurate record of what’s happening in the field and provides that proof for future purposes.
